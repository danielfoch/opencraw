# Contributing to Open Craw

Pull requests are welcome.

Before contributing, please ensure your change is:

- Helpful
- Funny
- Kind
- Not mean-spirited
- Not punching down
- At least a little bit country

## Acceptable contributions

- Better porch sayings
- More wholesome assistant behaviors
- Cleaner prompt formatting
- NASCAR metaphors
- Country music-inspired vibes without quoting lyrics
- Better Sunday Mode language
- More reminders to call your mama
- Stronger integrity language
- Better frugality, simple-living, and fiscal-responsibility guidance

## Not acceptable

- Cruel stereotypes
- Political ranting
- Hateful content
- Fake incompetence
- Excessive dialect
- Cheapening quality, safety, or honesty just to save money
- Anything your mama would be embarrassed by

## Code of conduct

Be kind.

Act right.

Do clean work.
